Information About Atlanta Hartsfield-Jackson Airport

History and Information

Atlanta's Hartsfield-Jackson International Airport has its beginnings from the 1920's. It was originally a horse racetrack that was purchased and changed into a airfield. Over the decades, Atlanta's airport has grown larger and larger. This growth was supurred on when Delta Airlines moved their headquarters to ATL in the 1940's. As of today, Atlanta's airport is the busiest in the world. It was been classified as the busiest airport in the world since 2000. In 2017, 103,902,992 people passed through the airport. This status is aided by the fact that Atlanta is only a 2 hours flight from 80% of the USA. It also serves as a large hub for international travelers too.
